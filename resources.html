🏪 What to Look For

When visiting a garden center, look for:

  • Healthy plants with vibrant color and no yellow leaves
  • Plants labeled with hardiness zone information
  • Staff who can answer questions about local growing conditions
  • Organic soil amendments and fertilizers

📅 Best Time to Shop

Timing your nursery visits:

  • Early spring for the best selection of perennials
  • Late spring for annual flowers and vegetables
  • Fall for trees, shrubs, and spring-blooming bulbs
  • Weekday mornings for personalized attention from staff

💡 Shopping Tips

Get the most out of your visit:

  • Bring your zip code to verify plant hardiness zones
  • Ask about native plants adapted to your region
  • Check return policies before purchasing
  • Look for end-of-season sales for great deals

🌱 After You Buy

Ensure success with your new plants:

  • Get a personalized care guide for each plant
  • Plant within a few days of purchase when possible
  • Water thoroughly after planting
  • Keep tags for future reference and care needs
